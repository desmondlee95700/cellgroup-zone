## Style Prompt

Energetic youth-group game explainer with a dark rec-room stage, chunky instructional typography, bright physical props, and fast playful motion. The frame should feel like a live activity diagram rather than a slide deck: players move, props react, labels snap into place, and action moments get bursts/trails.

## Colors

- Background: #101318
- Stage panel: #191f27
- Ink: #f7f1e8
- Muted text: #b9c0c9
- Action red: #ff4d3d
- Warm yellow: #ffd166
- Teal team: #22d3c5
- Blue team: #4f8cff
- Balloon pink: #ff5da8
- Balloon green: #5bd46d

## Typography

- Display: "Archivo Black", heavy uppercase for game names and action calls.
- Body: "IBM Plex Serif", readable instructional copy with human warmth.
- Labels: "Space Mono", compact counters, team names, and prop labels.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use generic circles as the only player representation.
- Do not use centered card slides as the main composition.
- Do not rely on purple-blue neon gradients.
- Do not let instruction text overpower the action diagrams.
